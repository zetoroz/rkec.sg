What we do

One contractor for the whole job.

From stripping the old floor to the final coat of paint — you deal with one team, start to finish.

HDB Renovation

Flooring, walls & ceilings, waterproofing, tiling and paint — full or partial reno for your HDB flat or condo, done by one team.

Flooring

Vinyl, laminate, herringbone, SPC and tile flooring — removal of old floor, sub-floor levelling, and supply & install.

Handyman

The small jobs that pile up — patching, drilling, mounting, silicone, door & hinge fixes, leaks and odd repairs around the home.

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Deep clean before you move in or hand back keys — floors, toilets, kitchen, windows and grilles, scrubbed and ready for inspection.

Fixture Fixing

Lights, fans, aircon trunking, taps, sinks, toilet bowls, curtain rails and TV brackets — supplied or yours, mounted and tested.

Taobao Goods Assembly

Flat-pack wardrobes, shelves, beds and imported Taobao furniture — unpacked, assembled, levelled and anchored to the wall safely.

Painting & Tiling

Full repaint, touch-ups and feature walls, plus floor & wall tiling and marble — proper prep, clean lines, level grout.

Ceiling & Drywall

False ceilings, cove and L-box lighting, partition walls and drywall — built, taped, skimmed and ready for paint.

Plumbing

Taps, sinks, basins, toilet bowls, water heaters and concealed pipework — supply, replace, re-route and fix leaks.

Demolition & Hacking

Hacking of walls, floors and old fittings, plus full strip-out — with debris cleared and disposed, ready for the new build.
